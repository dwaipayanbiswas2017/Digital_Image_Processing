Experiment-6

A. Write and execute programs for frequency domain filtering.
a. Apply FFT on a given image of your choice.
b. Perform low pass and high pass filtering in frequency domain.
c. Apply IFFT to reconstruct the image.

B. Apply wavelet transform on a given image.
a. Apply wavelet transform at different level of decomposition.
b. Reconstruct image by applying inverse wavelet transform.
c. Show that wavelet transform can be used to remove noise.
